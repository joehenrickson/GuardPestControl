What To Expect For Each Service



After scheduling an appointment, a licensed and trained Guard Pest Control technician will come to your home to complete an inspection to determine the best course of action to eliminate the pests on your property using the most current Integrated Pest Management methods.

You will be provided a comprehensive report addressing your pest issues, conducive conditions, and treatment plan. This report will make suggestions to correct favorable environmental conditions that are conducive to pests in our greater Houston environment.



Your technician will apply treatment as necessary to combat any of the invasive pests, specifically targeting areas of high activity in order to eliminate pests effectively.

Resolution of conducive conditions and appropriate treatment applications are an integral part of safe and effective pest extermination.

Broadcast sprays and heavy over applications applied by untrained or inexperienced technicians and pest control companies may briefly eliminate a pest issue but are damaging and dangerous for your family, home, and our environment.

Our technicians are experienced, licensed, and trained and committed to the highest standards of pest management methods.

Pest Control is science and Guard Pest Control is further committed to ongoing education ensuring our methods remain on the forefront of industry best practices and standards.



Customers protected by our Home Guard Protection Plans are assured ongoing pest free environments with targeted bi-monthly service to ensure pest activity has ceased in and around your home, conducive conditions are regularly identified and resolved, and damaging, unsafe, and unhealthy pests are eliminated.
